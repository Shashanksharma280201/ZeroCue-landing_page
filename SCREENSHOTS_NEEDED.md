## Quick Add Instructions

1. Take screenshots from the actual apps running on your devices
2. Use a tool like Figma, Photoshop, or online mockup generators to place screenshots in device frames
3. Export as high-quality PNG files
4. Place in the `public/` directory
5. The landing page will automatically display them

## Temporary Placeholders

Currently, the landing page uses gradient placeholders. Once you add the actual screenshots with the filenames above, they will automatically be displayed.
